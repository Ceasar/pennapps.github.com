So like, what is PennApps?

PennApps is the University of Pennsylvania's biannual, weekend-long hackathon. Talented student programmers team up in groups of up to four to build amazing applications. The weekend culminates in a demo session, where an impressive panel of judges awards prizes to the top hacks. Over the course of the hackathon, students are exposed to new technologies and have a chance to take their skills outside the classroom-- not to mention the free food and giveaways. PennApps is open to all current undergraduate and graduate students, and attracts participants from across the east coast. Curious? Check out our video.
